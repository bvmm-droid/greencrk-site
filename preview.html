Our Story

Family Owned Since 1983.

Babylon Village Meat Market was founded by Lenny and Barbara Popp right here on Deer Park Avenue. For over 40 years, we've been the neighborhood's butcher — cutting every piece to order, dry-aging our beef in-house, and treating every customer like family.

We're not a supermarket. We're a real butcher shop — where the person behind the counter knows your name, knows your order, and takes pride in every cut. That's what Our Block to Your Table means to us.
